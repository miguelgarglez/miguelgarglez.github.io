# Recruiter Profile Intake

Use this checklist when refining Miguel's public profile context for chat, recruiter conversations, or CV-facing copy.

## Impact and evidence

- Which 2-3 outcomes are the clearest proof of impact, preferably with metrics, scope, or adoption?
- Which project or initiative best shows ownership from problem to outcome?
- Which example best demonstrates improvement in quality, efficiency, accessibility, or developer experience?

## Leadership and collaboration

- Which examples best show leadership without formal authority?
- Where has Miguel influenced technical decisions, product direction, or the way a team works?
- What kind of teammates bring out his best work?
- Which team behaviors or cultural anti-patterns make him less effective?

## Career direction

- What role scope does he want next: senior IC with broader influence, tech lead, or people-adjacent technical leadership?
- What does 'technical leadership with business influence' mean in practical terms over the next 2-4 years?
- How much emphasis should the profile place on mentoring, coaching, and helping other engineers grow?

## Role and company fit

- Which industries, product problems, or company stages are most attractive right now?
- Which environments help him grow fastest: platform work, product delivery, ambiguity, mentoring, stakeholder alignment, or scaling quality?
- Are there role types or company patterns that should be avoided in profile copy, or should the narrative stay purely positive?

## Work setup

- Should the profile mention preferences around remote, hybrid, onsite, travel, or timezone overlap?
- Is there any availability or interview-process detail that should be explicitly represented?
